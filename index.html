The Universe

A house with no doors. A fire that remembers.

Somewhere beyond what the eye can reach, a universe sits inside a globe on a desk. Inside it, twelve planets orbit a false star. Civilizations rise. The corruption finds them. They fall. The cycle turns. It has always turned.

The Aurelon universe spans novels, campaign settings, and the spaces between — a shared cosmology where every story is connected by gates that open between worlds, a corruption that learns from everything it consumes, and a cycle that is older than anything living inside it.

The walls are thinner now. The hand that built them trembles.
