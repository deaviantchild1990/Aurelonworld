Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This policy explains what aurelonuniverse.com and its free text adventure, The Descent, collect, and what we do with it. It is written to be read, not to hide things.

The short version
  • The game runs in your browser. Your progress is saved on your own device and never sent to us.
  • We only receive information when you choose to claim an ending or submit the witness form.
  • We never collect your IP address or precise location, and we use no advertising or tracking cookies.
  • We do not sell your data. Ever.

What we collect, and when

While you play

Your game progress is stored locally in your browser (localStorage): the current room, inventory, prophecy fragments, and which endings you have seen. It stays on your device. We cannot see it.

When you claim an ending

If you reach one of the game's endings and choose to submit a claim, our server records:

  • the ending you reached and your rank for it;
  • basic run statistics (turn count, prophecy fragments found, shards, and whether certain endings were completed);
  • your country, an approximate region derived by our infrastructure provider, never your precise location or IP address;
  • your browser's user-agent string (the standard identifier browsers send with every request);
  • the time of the claim;
  • if you reach the Sleeping God ending, the name you typed for it.

When you submit the witness form

If you choose to join the witness circle, we collect:

  • your email address, so we can contact you about preread access;
  • your Discord handle, a proposed character name, and a message, all optional;
  • the claim it is attached to.

Analytics

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is privacy-first: it uses no cookies, does not fingerprint visitors, and reports only aggregate page statistics.

What we do not collect

  • No IP addresses are stored.
  • No precise or GPS location.
  • No tracking or advertising cookies.
  • No third-party ad networks.
  • We never sell, rent, or trade your information.

How we use what we collect

  • To rank ending claims and run the witness / preread program.
  • To contact witnesses about prereleasing the novels.
  • To understand, in aggregate, how the game is played so we can improve it.

Who processes your data

We keep things minimal. Your data may pass through:

  • GitHub Pages: hosts the website's files.
  • Cloudflare: runs the claim/state server (a Worker and key-value store) and the privacy-first analytics.
  • Discord: claim and witness notifications are sent to a private channel the author monitors.

These providers process data on our behalf. We do not share it with anyone else.

How long we keep it

Claim and witness records are kept for as long as the witness program runs. You can ask us to delete yours at any time (see Contact).

Your choices

  • To play without sending anything, simply don't submit a claim or the witness form. The game is fully playable, offline and anonymously.
  • To clear your local progress, use the in-game "new game" button or clear your browser's site data.
  • To request deletion of a claim or witness submission, contact us with your claim ID.

Children

The Descent contains dark themes and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date at the top. Material changes will be noted on the site.

Contact

Questions, or want your data deleted? Reach the author on our Discord, or email privacy@aurelonuniverse.com.
